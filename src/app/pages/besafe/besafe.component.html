Be Safe

As a tech enthusiast, I have always been passionate about creating solutions to real-world problems. When my girlfriend expressed her concerns about safety and the fear of sexual harassment and violence against women, it sparked an idea in me to create a safety app that would help women feel more secure.

Together with my best friend Mayuri Wandhe , I developed the app 'Be Safe' Our main goal was to design an application that would focus on personal safety and provide women with the essential features they need to feel secure. We included GPS tracking, emergency numbers, and directions to safe areas, among other features.

What sets our app apart is the alert button that sends alerts with your location to your loved ones in a situation of distress. We also added a feature that allows users to send alerts even if their phone is switched off by shaking it for three seconds.

Our aim was to create an app that would empower women to live freely and confidently. We believe that safety should not be a concern that holds women back from traveling alone or with friends. By developing this safety app, we hope to make a positive impact on the lives of women and their loved ones.

Additionally, we realized that this app could be beneficial to nannies and children. We wanted to provide a tool that would give parents peace of mind and allow them to know that their loved ones are safe wherever they are.

Overall, we are proud to have developed this safety app as a side project, and we hope it will make a difference in the lives of people who use it.
